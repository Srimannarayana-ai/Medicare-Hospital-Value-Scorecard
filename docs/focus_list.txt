Focus list - what it means

The Focus list page in Power BI keeps hospitals that are not
"Ok / review later".

Expected counts (current build):
- 469 hospitals total on the page
- 51  High spend / low experience
- 418 Low value (bottom 20%)

Rules
1) High spend / low experience
   MSPB > 1.10 and Experience_Star <= 2

2) Low value (bottom 20%)
   Value_Score is in the lowest fifth among hospitals that have a score

The full hospital_value sheet still has every hospital, including
"Ok / review later". That is normal. The Focus list page filters those out.
